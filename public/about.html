About Ayupathya

About Ayupathya Aahar Pvt Ltd

Ayupathya Aahar Pvt Ltd is dedicated to promoting Ayurveda-based nutrition and lifestyle for holistic health. We offer natural food products and consultations designed by Ayurvedic experts to restore balance and vitality naturally.

Our Team

Our team includes Ayurvedic Doctor Dr. Pranav Kumar and Founder Hariom Singh, supported by wellness specialists focused on spreading awareness of Ayurvedic nutrition.

Our Journey

Founded in 2023, Ayupathya Aahar Pvt Ltd began its journey to revive Ayurvedic food habits. In a short time, we’ve served 500+ patients, hosted health camps, and tested products like Barley Saktu and Wholesome Daliya.

Our Core Values
  • Authenticity – Rooted in Ayurveda
  • Quality – Natural and chemical-free
  • Integrity – Transparent and honest
  • Wellness – Holistic health for all
Partnership

We partner with local farmers, Ayurvedic doctors, and distributors to bring traditional, natural food to every Indian household.
