Privacy Policy

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except:

We use cookies to enhance user experience, monitor website performance, and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability or restrict processing.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
